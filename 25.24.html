Patch 25.24 notes

The last patch of 2025 is here!

Welcome to the last patch of the year, 25.24!

While this may be the last patch of the year, there's still a lot to look forward to! As a quick heads up, this won't be our last balance pass of the year either, expect a sizable micropatch in mid-December, all of which will be updated and noted here.

On the gameplay side of things, we have a relatively lighter patch of buffing and nerfing various outliers. The real shakeup comes in January with the new season start, so get ready for a lot more follow-up coming soon! We've also got quite a few changes for Arena and ARAM Mayhem (including a few new augments for ARAM), so be sure to check em out!

Outside of direct gameplay changes we've got WASD coming to live (in limited queues), the last patch of the 2025 Season 3 ranked split, some wonderful winter skins, improvements to champ select, the return of your shop, and more, so make sure to read more down below for all the details!
